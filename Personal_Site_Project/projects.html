Additive-Manufacturing-Technician

Project 4 : Prostetic

We can create custom prosthetic limbs for individual patients. These 3D-printed limbs are lighter and more comfortable. This process also saves time and reduces costs compared to traditional methods.

Project 5 : Aerospace

The aerospace industry uses 3D printing to make strong, lightweight parts like fuel nozzles. This technology allows for complex designs that can't be made with regular methods, improving performance. It also speeds up production and streamlines the supply chain.

Project 6 : Dental Implants

We can quickly producing custom dental implants and crowns. These personalized solutions improve patient comfort and fit. The process also reduces material waste and shortens the fitting time.
